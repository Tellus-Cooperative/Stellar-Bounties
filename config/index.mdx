# Configuration Guidelines

## Repository Allowlist Criteria

Repositories must meet the following criteria to be added to the allowlist:

1. **Public Goods**: Not primarily for-profit companies' core products
2. **Community Supported**: Used by companies but maintained as public goods
3. **Stellar Ecosystem Relevance**: Directly related to Stellar/Soroban development
4. **Dependency Weight**: Considered by how many people depend on the repository
5. **Maintenance Burden**: Historical issue volume indicates ongoing maintenance needs

## Sponsor Approval Process

New sponsors must:
- Provide clear contact information
- Specify monthly budget limits
- Agree to payout terms and conditions
- Be approved by Stellar Development Foundation

## Reviewer Requirements

Reviewers must:
- Have maintainer access to target repositories
- Demonstrate expertise in the relevant technology area
- Commit to 48-hour review SLA
- Follow established review guidelines

## Payout Calculation

Base payout = category base × difficulty multiplier

Example: Wallet Kit (2000 XLM) × Intermediate (1.5) = 3000 XLM
